Frequently asked questions

Clear answers for diligence, implementation, and day-to-day workflow decisions.

What is Quantura?

Quantura is a decision-intelligence platform that unifies forecasting, technical context, macro-aware news, options structure, and shared research workflows so teams can move from signal to action faster.

Who should use Quantura?

Quantura is built for active investors, research desks, founders, and operators who require repeatable decision systems, auditability, and rigorous scenario framing.

What is included in Weekly Brief vs Deep Forecast?

Weekly Brief provides recurring market context and prioritized setups. Deep Forecast is a paid, asset-specific package with probability bands, scenario diagnostics, risk triggers, and execution guidance.

Which forecasting methods are supported?

Quantura supports Meta Prophet workflows, TimeMixer alternatives, and AWS SageMaker Canvas baselines. Runs are persisted so assumptions, quantiles, and model behavior can be reviewed longitudinally.

Can I upload my own prediction CSV?

Yes. Uploads are workspace-scoped and support previewing, plotting, renaming, sharing, and deletion. This enables clean handoff from external model pipelines into Quantura analysis.

Can I save, reload, and share past runs?

Yes. Forecast and screener runs are versionable workspace assets you can reload, compare, and share with collaborators.

Do alerts and notifications run in real time?

Watchlists and price alerts are continuously evaluated from connected feeds. Delivery depends on device permissions and network conditions, and events are recorded in workspace history for traceability.

How does Quantura handle security and privacy?

Quantura applies authenticated access, encrypted transport, role-based permissions, and workspace-level controls. See the Privacy Policy for retention, transfer, and data-rights details.

Can I cancel a paid plan?

Yes. Plans can be canceled under the billing terms listed in Pricing and Terms. Cancellation affects future billing and does not reverse already-rendered services.

Does Quantura execute trades automatically?

No. Quantura is analytics and decision-support software. It is not a broker-dealer or investment adviser; all execution decisions remain with the user.

Can I export my research artifacts?

Yes. Forecasts, screener outputs, and upload artifacts can be exported for external BI, validation, compliance review, and reporting workflows.

Where do I request support or enterprise help?

Use the Contact page for onboarding, integration help, and enterprise forecasting engagements. Support generally responds within one business day.
