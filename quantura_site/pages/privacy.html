Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 11, 2026. This policy explains what data Quantura collects, why we collect it, and how you can manage or delete your information.

Privacy highlights

Account data is required for sign-in and workspace sync.
Analytics events improve reliability and product experience.
Forecasts, uploads, and orders are stored in your workspace.
You can request deletion of your account data.

1. Information we collect

We collect account identifiers (email, auth provider), usage telemetry, billing/order records, and files you upload for forecasting and analysis.

2. How we use data

Data is used to deliver features, secure your account, process transactions, generate analytics, and improve service quality.

3. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with service providers needed for hosting, storage, notifications, payments, and support.

4. Security

We use access controls, encrypted transport, and role-based authorization. No system is fully risk free; protect your credentials and use strong passwords.

5. Retention

Data is retained while your account is active and for limited periods for compliance, fraud prevention, and incident investigation.

6. Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion by contacting us. We may require identity verification before fulfilling requests.

7. Cookies and local storage

Quantura uses cookies/local storage for sign-in state, feature controls, preferences, and product analytics. You can manage consent in the app.
