Screener help

Quantura follows a professional multi-group filtering workflow: start with Descriptive scope, tighten Fundamental quality, confirm Technical participation, then add News/ETF structure filters.

Descriptive: exchange, index, sector, geography, market cap profile.
Fundamental: valuation, yield, profitability, leverage, analyst consensus.
Technical: price/volume pressure and short-float context.
News: earnings timing, target dislocation, IPO age windows.
Technical analysis introduction

Use technical filters for confirmation, not prediction. Prioritize setups where trend, momentum, and participation agree with your fundamental thesis.
