Introducing the Quantura Ticker Terminal

Published February 10, 2026

Quantura started as a forecasting workflow. The hard part wasn’t fitting a model, it was keeping research organized: chart, forecast, indicators, news, options, and saved runs.

Open the terminal Request Deep Forecast
Interactive charting (candles + overlays)
Saved forecasts you can re-plot
Indicator charts backed by Finta
Firebase functions + Firestore storage

What changed

The terminal centers everything around one object: the ticker. Load it once, and every panel downstream is ready to run: forecasting, technical indicators, news, and options.

Forecast runs are stored in Firestore with quantile rows. You can pull any prior run back, overlay it on the chart, and compare Prophet vs IBM TinyTimeMixer results side-by-side.

How to use it

Open `/forecasting` and load your ticker
Run a forecast with bands and save it automatically
Toggle indicators and graph them under the chart
Use Saved forecasts to plot prior runs instantly
