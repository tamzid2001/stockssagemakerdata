Frequently asked questions

Everything you need before running production research workflows.

What is Quantura?

Quantura is a market intelligence platform that combines forecasting, technical context, news, options analysis, and workspace workflows so teams can move from idea to decision with less friction.

Who should use Quantura?

Quantura is designed for active investors, research teams, founders, and operators who need structured, repeatable analysis rather than ad hoc chart checks.

What is included in Weekly Brief vs Deep Forecast?

Weekly Brief is your recurring market intelligence layer. Deep Forecast is a paid, asset-specific deliverable with forecast bands, scenario planning, risk triggers, and action-oriented commentary.

Which forecasting methods are supported?

You can run Meta Prophet workflows, TimeMixer alternatives, and an AWS SageMaker Canvas baseline for comparison. Forecast runs are saved so you can revisit assumptions and outcomes over time.

Can I upload my own prediction CSV?

Yes. Uploads are tied to your workspace and can be previewed, plotted, renamed, shared, or deleted with full CRUD controls. This supports handoffs between external model pipelines and Quantura workflows.

Can I save, reload, and share past runs?

Yes. Forecast runs and screener outputs are persisted in your workspace so you can reload them later, compare versions, and share with collaborators for review.

Do alerts and notifications run in real time?

Watchlists and price alerts are tracked continuously from connected data feeds. Notification delivery depends on client permissions and service availability, and all alert events are logged to your workspace history.

How does Quantura handle security and privacy?

Quantura uses authenticated access, encrypted transport, role-based permissions, and workspace-level access controls. See the Privacy Policy for full data handling details.

Can I cancel a paid plan?

Yes. Paid plans can be canceled according to the billing terms listed in Pricing and Terms. Cancellation changes future billing and does not retroactively reverse already rendered services.

Does Quantura execute trades automatically?

No. Quantura is an analytics and decision-support platform. It does not operate as a broker-dealer or investment adviser, and all final trade decisions remain with the user.

Can I export my research artifacts?

Yes. Forecasts, screen outputs, and upload-related artifacts can be downloaded and reused in external BI, model validation, and reporting workflows.

Where do I request support or enterprise help?

Use the Contact page for implementation support, enterprise onboarding, and custom forecasting engagements. Quantura support typically replies within one business day.
